Assalamualaikum… I hope you are doing well. I don't know how you are feeling these days, but I truly pray that you are safe, happy, and at peace.

You were the one who taught me how to smile again. You helped me reconnect with people. You made me feel understood in ways I had never felt before. Because of you, I grew. Because of you, I became better.

I don't know what the future holds, but I sincerely wish that one day we could meet again without anger, without pain — just with peace. Even if it's just once.

If possible… please forgive me.

No matter what happens, I will always pray for your happiness and success.

Forever grateful for you...
